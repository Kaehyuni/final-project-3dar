Biology Learning Resources

These resources are curated to support BIOL 1406 students. They focus on visual explanations, short videos, and clear navigation to help make biology easier to understand.

How to Use These Resources

Each topic includes three videos and three websites. The videos are short and visual, while the websites provide diagrams and explanations without overwhelming textbook style reading.

These resources work best when paired with the 3D and AR models found in the AR Lab.
