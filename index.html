About BioBridge

Many students struggle with biology because it is often taught through dense textbooks and static diagrams. BioBridge was created to change that experience.

As a Biology Tutor and Academic Coach, I work one-on-one with students to break down complex topics like cell structure, membrane transport, and protein synthesis using visual explanations and interactive tools.

My Inspiration

My passion for visual learning began in 7th grade with my science teacher, Mr. Andy Thomason. He introduced our class to augmented reality as a way to explore scientific concepts beyond the textbook.

“Seeing biology through AR made abstract ideas feel real. We weren’t just reading about cells — we were exploring them.”

That experience shaped how I support students today. BioBridge continues that same philosophy: learning is most effective when students can see, explore, and interact with what they study.
